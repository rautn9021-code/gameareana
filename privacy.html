🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority — we keep it simple and transparent

📌 Overview

Welcome to BubbleVerse ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website or play our Bubble Sort Puzzle game. We believe in minimal data collection and maximum transparency. Our games are designed to be fully client-side, meaning your actions stay in your browser.

🍃 Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information. No names, emails, IP addresses, or browsing history are tracked or stored. Our website uses no analytics scripts, no third-party tracking pixels, and no cookies for identification.

✨ Zero Data Collection Guarantee — You can play our games without worrying about privacy. The Bubble Sort Puzzle runs entirely locally within your browser. We never send any game state or personal data to any server.

However, like almost every website, your browser may automatically share anonymous technical data (e.g., browser type, operating system) with our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) for security and performance reasons. This data is not accessible to us and is only used to serve the webpage correctly.

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. Our website does not store any persistent identifiers on your device. The game does not use localStorage or IndexedDB to remember your progress. If you refresh the page, the game state resets — ensuring no hidden data lingers.

Cloudflare, our hosting provider, may set a strictly necessary cookie for security purposes (e.g., to mitigate bot attacks). These are functional cookies and contain no personal information.

🎮 How the Bubble Sort Game Works

The Bubble Sort Puzzle is a pure front-end JavaScript application. When you play:

  • ✅ All bottle/pour logic happens inside your browser's memory.
  • ✅ No game state is transmitted over the internet.
  • ✅ No external APIs are called (except loading the page itself).
  • ✅ Undo & reset functions are local only.

This means you can play completely offline after the page loads — your privacy is fully protected.

🔗 Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (like GitHub or social media). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before interacting with them.

👶 Children's Privacy

BubbleVerse is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is no risk of exposing children's personal information. Parents and guardians can rest assured that playing our games is completely private and secure.

🌍 International Users

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare's global network. If you access BubbleVerse from outside your country, your connection is routed through Cloudflare's edge servers. However, because we collect no personal data, GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy regulations do not impose additional obligations on us — we are naturally compliant by design (no data = no risk).

📧 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect improvements or legal clarifications. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this page from time to time. Continued use of the site means you accept any revised policy.
